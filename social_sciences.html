Geography

Geography is the science of describing the Earth, as it studies the topography and population distribution and takes care of the study of the earth and its characteristics and natural phenomena (earthquakes, volcanoes, hurricanes and other phenomena that affect the earth) in a more precise sense, geography studies the coherence of the universe, just as geography interferes with other sciences such as economics, general sciences and geology, and this Science is concerned with the study of climate, human distribution, regions, plants and animals, agriculture, industry and commerce.

History

History is a science that provides complete knowledge of the history of all civilizations, including Arab, Islamic, and modern world civilizations. It also provides an account of all the historical events that occurred in various countries, as well as the sciences. However, the discipline of history focuses primarily on political aspects and experiences.

Political science

Political science Political science is one of the branches of sociology, and it studies political theory, describing and analyzing political systems and their political behavior and their social and international implications. And international relations between nations and their history,

Law

Law It is a social science that studies a set of general rules and laws, which in turn work to regulate social relations between members of society, provided that all members of society adhere to them without differentiating them..

Social sciences

Social sciences it is a branch of science related to human behavior that includes its social and cultural aspects, and is sometimes used to refer to psychology, sociology, politics, economics, history, and law, and often includes social and economic geography.

Psychology

Psychology is the scientific study of behavior, the ability to control and predict it, and it is the study of thinking, the mind and personality, and work to analyze them accurately and identify the different patterns of it, and apply what has been studied on the ground, in order to know the psychological problems that a person faces, and strive to solve them And treat it from the roots.
