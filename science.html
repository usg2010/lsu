Physics

It is the science that studies basic concepts, such as sound, light and motion, and it is the science that attempts to understand the laws of nature and the relationship between energy and matter. Physics deals with particles to black holes and the general structure of the universe. Mathematical formulas are used to try to explain theories and make predictions.

Biology

Biology is a branch of science specialized in the study of plants, animals, bacteria, viruses and all microorganisms, and the aim of it is to know the nature of these organisms' life and existence, how they reproduce, their diseases, and methods of treatment.

Chemistry

chemistry is the science that studies matter and the changes that occur to it, specifically by studying its properties, structure, structure, behavior, interactions and what it causes through it. Chemistry studies the atoms and the bonds that occur between them.

Computer science

Computer science major is interested in studying the mechanisms of computers in addressing and solving complex problems, by using mathematical analysis and algorithms using different programming languages, and it is also interested in studying how to protect programs and networks. The major focuses on Software and Data Systems, Network and Information Security.

Biochemistry

Biochemistry is one of the branches of natural sciences and it is specialized in studying the chemical composition of cell parts in various living organisms, whether they are simple organisms such as (bacteria, fungi and algae) or as complex as humans, animals and plants.

Math

Mathematics is the science of studying abstract structures using logic, mathematical proofs and mathematical notation, and it is the backbone of most modern sciences that have contributed to the development of mankind over the past centuries, especially in physics and chemistry, It became one of the modern applied sciences intertwined with all other sciences, representing a prominent position in scientific studies in colleges.
