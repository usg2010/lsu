is how to manage pharmacies, prepare medicines, and provide advice to patients. It is one of the specializations of the College of Applied Medical Sciences that is concerned with teaching medical imaging methods to diagnose diseases and aid in their treatment, and the specialty is divided into several sub-departments, each of which has its own advantages and uses in the field of disease diagnosis such as magnetic resonance imaging, CT scans, x-rays, nuclear medicine, and vessels And interventional radiology and ultrasound.

Psychiatry

Psychiatry is a branch of medicine that specializes in the study, diagnosis, prevention, and treatment of mental disorders. This includes various distortions related to mood, behavior, perception and understanding.

Veterinary Medicine

veterinary medicine aims primarily to provide its students with theoretical and practical skills related to animals, their breeding, and their health, as the main task of the veterinarian is to diagnose diseases, injuries, or any other disorders that animals are exposed to. After the diagnosis, the doctor will prescribe the necessary medications, treatments, and surgery.

Anesthesiology

Anesthesiology is one of the most important branches of medicine. It is concerned with providing health care before, during and after surgery. The importance of this specialty comes from the inability to perform any surgical work without the presence of an anesthesiologist in the operating room.

Pediatrics

Pediatrics specialization is one of the modern medical branches concerned with the health care of children of all ages, and therefore pediatrics may specialize in studying the psychological and physical developments of the child, and it may include treating various diseases and disabilities for children, and providing all medical and health care, whether they are newborns or From childhood through adolescence.

Dentistry

Dentistry is one of the distinguished medical specialties that cannot be dispensed with, as it specializes in diagnosing and treating various diseases that are associated with the teeth and all the tissues that surround them, the mouth, the jaws, and the face, and methods of prevention of these diseases to avoid infection and exacerbation of the condition, and this specialization needs to Special skills and requirements that enable the student to transfer in the academic stages to obtain a scientific degree in the study of dentistry.

Hearing and Speech Science

It is a specialist who specializes in hearing problems, how to discover them and evaluate their severity, and his functions include measuring the patient’s hearing, detecting various hearing impairment problems, and testing the appropriate stethoscope for the disease. Often times the audiologist and speech-language specialist partner with an otolaryngologist in the treatment and follow-up of the case.

General surgery

General surgery is a surgical specialty that focuses on the intra-abdominal organs and systems, including the esophagus, stomach, small intestine, colon, liver, pancreas, gallbladder, bile ducts and often the thyroid gland (depending on the availability of a surgeon specializing in head and neck surgery). It also includes diseases related to the skin, breasts, tissues and hernia.

Nursing

It is one of the medical specialties that takes care of the patient’s health and relieves his pain, and nursing is also considered one of the auxiliary specialties for the medical profession, where the nursing specialist assists the doctor in providing treatment methods to the patient, such as: needles and medicines, in addition to monitoring his health condition, and providing the doctor with the necessary medical examinations, And the health file for each patient.

Nutrition

Nutrition is one of the fields of ever-evolving science that researches developments in diet, and of course, with the aim of caring for people's physical health.
