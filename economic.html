Economic sciences

Economics is the method that contributes to making decisions about how to use limited resources to meet needs and desires. Economics is also defined as the science that studies the scarcity of human resources and how to use them. This specialization includes a wide range of study subjects such as recession and finance, wealth and others.

Business Administration

Business Administration in a way to qualify and train individuals to organize the work of companies or establishments, through several processes, including: direction, control, planning, and control, starting from preparing tax returns to supervising employees. Specialization Information pertaining to several areas; Such as administration, accounting, marketing, business decision-making, organization, and human resource management.

accounting

Financial Accounting is the recording of financial operations related to business, and it records economic events in a comprehensive and orderly manner. Accounting includes summarizing and analyzing those financial operations. ... as the accounting specialty is classified as one of the oldest fields concerned with financial matters, and it aims fundamentally to improve the performance of companies and institutions in the future.

Human Resources

The concept of human resources specialization refers to that administrative field that deals specifically with the human element in the department of work, that is, in all matters related to employees, including their entitlements, the employment of new people from them, the mechanism for conducting interviews, and others, so that specialists in this field work with a large group Of managers and executives in institutions or organizations, and their work is based on the strategic planning side, where their previous experiences are used to provide the best suggestions and ideas for changing employee policies.

Marketing

Marketing is one of the administrative sciences that is responsible for managing all activities carried out by a company, institution, or project for the purpose of promoting services or products and pushing consumers to buy, or for the purpose of branding among different classes of the public. Digital marketing is used in most parts of the world.

M I S

Management Information Systems, known as MIS, which are computerized systems designed to serve managers in the administrative organization, and it combines information technology, computer science, and management, and its goal is to build technological computer systems to help various institutions in carrying out their work, and perform many functions, Including: office assistance, carrying out accounting tasks, organizing meetings, and other matters that help institutions in the decision-making process.

finance

Financing is identifying the needs of individuals, organizations, companies, and monetary resources and determining how to collect and use them while taking into account the risks associated with their projects. As for investment, it is any economic activity that has a return to the owner directly or indirectly.
