One of the most important criteria that governs the choice of academic specialization at the university level is the desire and passion for specialization, ... and usually this great tendency towards a specialization appears in the student at early stages of study, such as a tendency to literary or scientific subjects, attachment to mathematics, physics, chemistry or his attachment to literature, music, or even His tendency is economics or political matters.

study in practical and financial terms:

And since university study is one of the most important stages of entering the labor market in the modern era, it is natural for the professional future of specialization to be a fundamental criterion in choosing university specialization in accordance with the requirements of the market, the supply and demand for job opportunities and the country's needs of a workforce in certain fields at the expense of other sectors.
